Chewing-gum collectors


A set of two solutions: a chewing-gum packaging and urbans collectors.

made out of sheet metal, paper and cardboard.

DISCIPLINES:Service design, Urban design

DURATION: 3 months

CLIENT:CITEO

YEAR: 2021

The problems tackled

Stocking

1

Collecting

2

The packaging allows users to stock the used chewing-gum.

Chewing-gum being made out of 60% of sugar is much smaller once chewed.

The boards incentivize people in order to prevent
chewing-gum from landing on the ground.

Maintenance can easily pull off the pieces of paper to collect the chewing-gum stucked on it.
This helps collecting the packaging as well

During the process, we have designed prototypes in order to better understand the proportions of the different objects.
