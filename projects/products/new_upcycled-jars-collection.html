The Upcycled Jars Collection

The Upcycled Jars Collection is collection of 3D-printed objects that screw on top of the jars we all have at home.

Date

2021

My role

Product design

Team size

Mano Silberzahn, Product Design ; (2 objects)

Upcycling existing jars and 3D-printing allow a better efficiency and cutting down shipping cost.

view in AR Android east

view in AR iOS east

Recycling glass uses up to 60% of the energy needed to create new one. Upcycling is a more efficient way to use the material we already have around.

My main goal for this project was to allow people to reuse directly their jars, by creating simple objects that are used everyday.

Objects for a lot of different needs

We intended to create various objects for the living room, bathroom or even kitchen.
Sustainable production

Recycled bioplastic that decomposes in industrial composters, locally 3D-printed

All objects are 3D-printed in the parisian region, and shipped to Europe-only.

The objects are made out of bioplastic recycled by European manufacturers, and can decompose in industrial composters.
