Sustainable scoring app

GREENER is an app that allows users to get access to knowledge about what they buy

Date

2020

My role

UX/UI Design

Duration

2 weeks

Team size

Only me

As part of my semester at ESCP Business School for the Option Entrepreneurship, we were asked to help an existing start-up. I chose to work as a UX/UI designer for them. They had an idea, but no app.

This is the app, followed by the landing page that I designed for them.
The initial goal

Designing a website for a product design studio, detailing the designers and product pages.

The founders had original sketches with 12 differents menus for the app and a LOT of functionalities they wanted to implement.

I spent a lot of time prioritizing the features that would be the most interesting for the users.

Branding

Choosing the logo

The founders originally didn’t want to modify their logo. A work was done to simplify it , and creating a clearer version of the app icon, which they didn’t have before.

Branding

Color

A survey was made for the app target in order to choose the color that inspired them most a sustainable app.

The majority chose the one that is now their primary color.

Landing page

Creating a simple landing page to better explain their product

A simple landing page was also created, as they both had some knowledge in coding. This allows them to have an online presence and explain the different features their product offers.
